Node-Based WorkflowConcept

A node-based workflow is a visual-programming approach where individual processing steps are represented as connected nodes on a canvas; ComfyUI applies this paradigm to AI image, video, 3D, and audio generation pipelines.

Connect models, processing steps, and outputs on a canvas where every decision is visible and every step is inspectable. Comfy — Professional Control of Visual AI - published by Comfy Org
