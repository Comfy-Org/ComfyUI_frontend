question: "How does the Team Plan work?"
order: 5
---

The Team Plan puts your whole team on **one shared credit pool.** Every member draws from the same balance, so you're not juggling separate subscriptions. Key things to know:

**One pool, shared.** Everyone generates against the same credit balance.

**Invite by email.** Add teammates, and resend or revoke access any time.

**Owners manage billing.** Assign owners who handle payment and buy top-ups for the team.

**Upgrade in place.** Move an existing workspace to a team and your workflows, models, and assets stay attached.

Choose your monthly credit commitment that fits your team. [Get started today.](https://cloud.comfy.org/?pricing=team)
