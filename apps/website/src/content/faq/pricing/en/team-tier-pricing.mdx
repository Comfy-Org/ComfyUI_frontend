---
question: "What do the Team plan tiers cost, and how does the discount work?"
order: 9
---

Team plans come in **five tiers from $200 to $2,500/month**, set by a credit-commit slider. A bigger monthly commit means a bigger discount: **annual plans go up to 20% off, monthly plans up to 10%**. The discount starts at the $400 tier (5% annual / 2.5% monthly) and scales from there.
