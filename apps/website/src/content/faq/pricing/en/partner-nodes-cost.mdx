---
question: "What are Partner Nodes, and do they cost extra?"
order: 14
---

Partner Nodes let you run proprietary models (like Nano Banana Pro) directly inside your workflow. They draw from the same credit pool as your subscription (no separate bill); how much each call costs depends on the model and parameters you set. These credits work across both Comfy Cloud and Comfy Desktop. [Read more about Partner Nodes](https://docs.comfy.org/tutorials/partner-nodes/overview).
