---
question: "What are the runtime and concurrency limits?"
order: 12
---

Each workflow has a max runtime of **30 minutes** on Standard and Creator, raised to **1 hour** on Pro. Jobs over the limit are cancelled automatically to keep the system fair and stable. You can queue up to **100 workflows** at once, and run **1 / 3 / 5** concurrently via API on Standard / Creator / Pro. If you need to increase your Team plan concurrency limit, seats or API rate limits, contact us [here](https://comfy-org.portal.usepylon.com/forms/team-plan-requests).
