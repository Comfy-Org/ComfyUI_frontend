---
question: "What's the difference between Standard, Creator, and Pro?"
order: 4
---

**Standard -** 30-min max runtime per workflow, 1 concurrent workflow via API. For individuals building workflows.

**Creator -** Everything in Standard plus the ability to **import your own models** (from CivitAI or Hugging Face) and run up to 3 workflows concurrently via API.

**Pro -** Everything in Creator plus **longer runtime (up to 1 hour)** per workflow and up to 5 concurrent workflows via API. For teams running Comfy in production.
