---
question: "Is Team pricing per-seat? Can I add a freelancer just for a project?"
order: 7
---

No. Team pricing is based on **your monthly credit commit**, not per-seat. Invite a freelancer, they draw from the shared credit pool while they're working, then remove them when the project wraps. **No charge for adding or removing people.** Member count is capped at **50** today; if you hit the cap, [contact support](https://portal.usepylon.com/comfy-org/forms/question) for additional seats.
