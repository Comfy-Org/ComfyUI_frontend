question: "What's a credit worth? How far does it go?"
order: 1
---

Credits map to GPU runtime, so mileage depends on the workflow. As a reference point, a five-second video* uses roughly **11 credits**, so Standard covers a few hundred per month, Creator about double that, and Pro enough for over a thousand.

\*Based on 5s videos using the Wan 2.2 Image-to-Video template at default settings (81 frames, 18fps, 640×640, 4-step sampler). Heavier models, higher resolutions, and the inclusion of Partner nodes use more.
